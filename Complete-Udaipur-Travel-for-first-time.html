First-Time Visitor's Guide

The Complete Udaipur
Travel Guide for First-Time Visitors

Often called the City of Lakes, Udaipur is known for its magnificent palaces, historic havelis, scenic boat rides, vibrant markets, and timeless charm. Whether you're visiting as a couple, with family, or as a solo traveler, Udaipur offers experiences that go far beyond sightseeing.

Why Visit Udaipur

Why Visit Udaipur?

Udaipur is consistently ranked among India's most beautiful cities for good reason. Unlike many destinations that focus on one type of experience, Udaipur combines history, culture, nature, food, and hospitality in a single destination.


The city offers stunning lakes and waterfront views, rich Mewar heritage, royal palaces and historic architecture, cultural performances and festivals, traditional Rajasthani cuisine, boutique shopping experiences, romantic settings for couples, and family-friendly attractions.

Getting There

Where is Udaipur Located?

Udaipur is located in southern Rajasthan and serves as the historic capital of the Mewar region. The city is well-connected by air, rail, and road.


Maharana Pratap Airport is approximately 22 km from the city center. Udaipur City Railway Station connects the city with major Indian destinations. Well-maintained highways connect Udaipur to Jaipur, Jodhpur, Ahmedabad, Mount Abu, and Delhi.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather, comfortable sightseeing, clear skies, and ideal boating conditions. Most first-time visitors should plan during these months.

Must-See Attractions

Top Attractions Every First-Time Visitor Should See

From royal palaces to serene lakes, these are the places no first-time visitor to Udaipur should miss.

01
Attraction 01

City Palace

The magnificent City Palace is Udaipur's most iconic attraction. Highlights include royal courtyards, historic museums, decorative balconies, and lake-view terraces.

Recommended Time: Allow at least 2–3 hours for your visit
02

Lake Pichola

Lake Pichola is the heart of the city. Don't miss boat rides, sunset views, Jag Mandir, and the waterfront ghats. It's one of the most photographed locations in Rajasthan.

Don't Miss: Boat rides and sunset views over the lake

Location: Near City Palace — easy to combine in one visit

Best For: Morning walks and peaceful evening outings

Don't Miss: The evening folk performance

Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace

Best For: One of the best sunsets in Rajasthan

Travel Tips

Practical Tips & Mistakes to Avoid

Start sightseeing early — major attractions are more enjoyable before crowds arrive. Wear comfortable footwear as many historic areas require walking. Carry cash for small purchases, as local markets may still prefer cash.


Plan sunset activities, as some of Udaipur's best experiences happen around sunset. Leave time for unplanned exploration — the city's hidden streets and lakeside corners often become trip highlights.

Trying to Cover Everything in One Day

Udaipur is best enjoyed at a relaxed pace.

Ignoring Local Food

Missing the Old City
