Where to Stay in Udaipur: A Guide for Every Type of Traveller

Choosing where to stay can significantly shape your experience of Udaipur. Known for its shimmering lakes, grand palaces, heritage havelis, and vibrant old-city charm, Udaipur offers a wide variety of accommodation options ranging from luxury resorts and heritage hotels to boutique stays and family-friendly properties.

Known for its shimmering lakes, grand palaces, heritage havelis, and vibrant old-city charm, Udaipur offers a wide variety of accommodation options ranging from luxury resorts and heritage hotels to boutique stays and family-friendly properties. But the best place to stay depends largely on your travel style.


Are you a first-time visitor hoping to stay near major attractions? A couple looking for a romantic getaway? A family seeking convenience and comfort? Or a traveler searching for an authentic heritage experience? This guide will help you find the ideal area and type of accommodation for your trip to Udaipur.

01

Best Area for First-Time Visitors: Lake Pichola & Old City

If it's your first trip to Udaipur, staying near Lake Pichola or within the Old City is often the most convenient choice.

Why Stay Here?
You'll be close to:

  • City Palace
  • Jagdish Temple
  • Bagore Ki Haveli
  • Gangaur Ghat
  • Traditional markets
  • Rooftop restaurants

The area allows visitors to explore many attractions on foot.

Best For: First-time visitors, International travelers, Heritage enthusiasts, Photographers

Things to Consider: The Old City's narrow lanes can become busy, particularly during peak tourist seasons.
02

Best Area for Couples: Near Lake Pichola

Udaipur is often called one of India's most romantic destinations, and Lake Pichola sits at the center of that reputation.

The lakeside atmosphere offers:

  • Sunset views
  • Rooftop dining
  • Boat rides
  • Heritage architecture
  • Romantic walking routes

Ideal For: Couples, Honeymooners, Anniversary trips, Romantic getaways

Many boutique hotels in Udaipur are located within this area, providing a more intimate and personalized experience compared to larger resorts.
03

Best Area for Families: Fateh Sagar Lake

Families often prefer staying around Fateh Sagar Lake.

Benefits:

  • Less crowded environment
  • Scenic surroundings
  • Wide roads
  • Easy transportation
  • Family-friendly atmosphere

Nearby attractions include:

  • Saheliyon Ki Bari
  • Fateh Sagar Promenade
  • Nehru Garden
  • Local cafés
Ideal For: Families with children, Multi-generational travelers, Long stays
04

Best Area for Luxury Travelers

Luxury travelers have multiple options in Udaipur.

These include:

  • Palace hotels
  • Lakeside resorts
  • Boutique luxury properties
  • Heritage mansions

Luxury accommodations often focus on:

  • Premium views
  • Fine dining
  • Personalized service
  • Exclusive experiences
However, many travelers today are increasingly choosing boutique hotels over large resorts because they offer a more personal connection to the destination.
05

Best Area for Heritage Lovers

One of the most unique aspects of Udaipur is its collection of historic havelis and heritage properties.

Staying in a heritage hotel allows travelers to experience:

  • Traditional Mewari architecture
  • Historic interiors
  • Cultural atmosphere
  • Personalized hospitality

Rather than simply visiting heritage attractions during the day, guests become part of the experience.

Best For: Cultural travelers, History enthusiasts, International visitors, Boutique travel seekers

Types of Hotels in Udaipur

When researching hotels in Udaipur, you'll encounter several accommodation categories.

Heritage Hotels

These are restored palaces, mansions, and historic havelis that preserve architectural and cultural heritage. Advantages: Unique character, Historic ambiance, Authentic local design, Cultural immersion.

Boutique Hotels

Boutique hotels focus on personalized service, distinctive design, smaller property size, and individual guest experiences. For many modern travelers, boutique hotels provide a more memorable stay than larger chain properties.

Luxury Resorts

These properties typically offer extensive facilities, spa services, multiple restaurants, and recreational activities. Suitable for travelers seeking a resort-style holiday.

Budget Hotels

Ideal for travelers prioritizing affordability and convenience.

What is the purpose of my trip?

Sightseeing, Relaxation, Romance, Cultural exploration

How important is location?

Staying closer to attractions can reduce transportation time.

Do I prefer a local experience?

Heritage and boutique hotels often provide a stronger connection to local culture.

Am I looking for personalized service?

Smaller properties frequently offer more attentive hospitality.
