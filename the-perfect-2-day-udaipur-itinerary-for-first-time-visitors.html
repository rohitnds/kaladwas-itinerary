The Perfect 2-Day Udaipur Itinerary for First-Time Visitors

Nestled among the Aravalli Hills, Udaipur is one of India's most enchanting destinations. Known as the City of Lakes, it combines royal palaces, historic havelis, scenic waterfronts, vibrant culture, and timeless Rajasthan hospitality.


For first-time visitors, the challenge is rarely finding things to do—it's deciding what to prioritize. The good news is that many of Udaipur's most iconic attractions are located close to one another, making it possible to experience the city's highlights in just two days. This carefully planned 2-day Udaipur itinerary helps you discover the best of the city without feeling rushed, while leaving enough time to soak in its unique atmosphere.

Day by Day

Your 2-Day Udaipur Itinerary

Day 1: Discover Udaipur's Royal Heritage and Lakes

8:00
AM
City Palace

8:00 AM – Start with City Palace

City Palace

Allow at least 2–3 hours

Begin your Udaipur journey at the magnificent City Palace. Overlooking Lake Pichola, this sprawling palace complex offers a fascinating introduction to the history of the Mewar dynasty. Wander through ornate courtyards, royal chambers, colorful mosaics, and museums that showcase centuries of royal heritage.

  • Mor Chowk
  • Badi Mahal
  • Crystal Gallery
  • Palace Museum
  • Lake viewpoints

11:00 AM – Visit Jagdish Temple

Jagdish Temple

Time Required: 30–45 minutes

Just a few steps from City Palace stands the historic Jagdish Temple. Built in 1651, this intricately carved temple remains one of Udaipur's most important religious landmarks. Its impressive architecture and spiritual atmosphere make it worth a short stop.

12:00 PM – Explore Udaipur's Old City

Udaipur's Old City

The narrow lanes surrounding City Palace offer a glimpse into the city's historic character. Unlike modern commercial districts, the Old City retains much of its original charm.

  • Traditional handicraft shops
  • Miniature painting studios
  • Local cafés
  • Historic havelis
  • Colorful markets

1:30 PM – Lunch with a View

Lunch with a View

Udaipur is known for its rooftop restaurants offering panoramic views of lakes and palaces. Enjoy a relaxed lunch while taking in views of Lake Pichola, Jag Mandir, and City Palace. This is one of the most memorable dining experiences in the city.

4:00 PM – Boat Ride on Lake Pichola

Boat Ride on Lake Pichola

No first trip to Udaipur is complete without experiencing Lake Pichola from the water. The lake's tranquil atmosphere provides a refreshing break from sightseeing.

  • City Palace
  • Jag Mandir
  • Lake Palace
  • Historic ghats

6:00 PM – Sunset at Gangaur Ghat

Gangaur Ghat

Gangaur Ghat is one of the most atmospheric places in Udaipur. As the sun sets behind the hills, locals gather along the waterfront while the lake reflects the city's historic skyline. For photographers and couples, this is one of the most romantic spots in the city.

7:00 PM – Evening Cultural Show at Bagore Ki Haveli

Bagore Ki Haveli

End your first day at Bagore Ki Haveli. Many travelers consider this the most authentic cultural experience in Udaipur.

  • Folk dances
  • Puppet shows
  • Traditional music
  • Rajasthan's artistic heritage

Day 2: Scenic Views, Local Experiences and Hidden Charm

8:00
AM
Fateh Sagar Lake

8:00 AM – Breakfast by Fateh Sagar Lake

Fateh Sagar Lake

Start your morning at Fateh Sagar Lake. Compared to Lake Pichola, it offers a more relaxed local atmosphere. Morning walks here provide beautiful views of the Aravalli Hills, calm lake waters, and local life.

10:00 AM – Explore Saheliyon Ki Bari

Saheliyon Ki Bari

This elegant garden was built for royal women and remains one of Udaipur's most peaceful attractions. It's an ideal stop for travelers interested in Rajasthan's royal lifestyle.

  • Marble fountains
  • Lotus pools
  • Landscaped gardens
  • Historic architecture

12:00 PM – Discover Local Markets

Local Markets

Spend a few hours exploring Udaipur's vibrant markets. Markets around Hathipole and Bapu Bazaar are particularly popular.

  • Handmade textiles
  • Silver jewelry
  • Leather products
  • Miniature paintings
  • Traditional handicrafts

2:00 PM – Relax and Enjoy Local Cuisine

Local Cuisine

Use the afternoon to sample traditional Rajasthani dishes. Food is an essential part of the Udaipur experience.

  • Dal Baati Churma
  • Gatte Ki Sabzi
  • Ker Sangri
  • Laal Maas

4:30 PM – Visit Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace

Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace

Head towards Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace for the most spectacular views in the city. Perched high above Udaipur, the palace offers panoramic views of Lake Pichola, Fateh Sagar Lake, Aravalli Hills, and the Udaipur skyline.

6:30 PM – Sunset Over Udaipur

Sunset Over Udaipur

Watching the sunset from Monsoon Palace is the perfect way to conclude your Udaipur itinerary. As the city lights begin to appear below and the lakes glow beneath the evening sky, you'll understand why Udaipur is considered one of India's most beautiful cities.

Key Takeaways

Key Takeaways

Two days are enough to experience Udaipur's major highlights.

City Palace and Lake Pichola should be at the top of every itinerary.

Bagore Ki Haveli offers one of the city's best cultural experiences.

Fateh Sagar Lake provides a more relaxed local atmosphere.

Monsoon Palace offers the most spectacular sunset views.

Heritage stays can enhance the overall Udaipur experience.
