Udaipur Travel Guide

When is the Best Time
to Visit Udaipur?

Known as the City of Lakes, Udaipur is one of Rajasthan's most visited destinations throughout the year. Weather conditions, festival seasons, tourist crowds, and hotel prices all change throughout the year — this month-by-month guide will help you decide the perfect time for your trip.

12 Months
Covered
Weather Overview

Udaipur Weather
at a Glance

Udaipur experiences three primary seasons — Winter (October–March) with pleasant weather and peak tourism, Summer (April–June) with hot days but fewer crowds, and Monsoon (July–September) with green landscapes and scenic beauty. Each season offers a different perspective of the city.


For most travelers, October to March offers the most pleasant weather and ideal sightseeing conditions. However, each season has its own advantages depending on your travel style and interests.

Month-by-Month Guide

Udaipur Month-by-Month Travel Guide

Every month in Udaipur offers a unique experience. Explore by season to find the best time for your trip.

October — 18°C – 33°C

Start of Peak Season

October marks the beginning of Udaipur's most popular tourism period. The weather becomes ideal for sightseeing, walking tours, and lake activities.

  • Temperature: 18°C – 33°C
  • Ideal for first-time visitors, families, and international tourists
  • Ideal for sightseeing, walking tours, and lake activities

November — Comfortable Weather

One of the Best Months to Visit

November offers nearly perfect travel conditions. Most attractions can be explored comfortably throughout the day.

  • Comfortable weather and clear skies
  • Excellent photography conditions
  • Pleasant evenings

December — Peak Season

Peak Travel Season

December is one of the busiest months in Udaipur. Visitors love the cool weather, holiday atmosphere, festive celebrations, and excellent sightseeing conditions.

  • Cool weather and festive celebrations
  • Holiday atmosphere
  • Book accommodations well in advance as demand increases significantly

January — 8°C – 25°C

Best for Sightseeing

January is one of the best months to explore Udaipur comfortably. Cool mornings, sunny days, and excellent visibility make it ideal for families, international travelers, heritage tourism, photography, and couples.

  • Temperature: 8°C – 25°C
  • Pleasant weather and excellent visibility for sightseeing
  • Comfortable boat rides
  • Peak tourist season — higher hotel rates

February — Perfect Balance

Perfect Balance of Weather and Crowds

February offers some of the most enjoyable travel conditions of the year. Many travelers consider February the best month overall for a trip to Udaipur.

  • Ideal for romantic getaways and honeymoon trips
  • Comfortably explore City Palace, Lake Pichola, Fateh Sagar Lake, Old City, and Monsoon Palace
  • Ideal for cultural experiences

March — Festival Season

Festival Season Begins

March marks the transition from winter to summer. It's ideal for photography, heritage walks, and cultural travelers. Temperatures begin rising toward the end of the month.

  • Pleasant mornings and longer daylight hours
  • Cultural events and festival celebrations
  • Ideal for photography, heritage walks, and cultural travelers

April — 20°C – 38°C

Warm but Manageable

Summer starts becoming noticeable. While afternoons can be hot, mornings and evenings remain enjoyable. Travelers looking for value often find April attractive.

  • Temperature: 20°C – 38°C
  • Smaller crowds and better hotel deals
  • Easier sightseeing access

May — 25°C – 42°C

Budget-Friendly Travel

May is one of the hottest months in Udaipur. Outdoor activities are best scheduled for early mornings and evenings. If you're looking for affordable hotels in Udaipur, May often offers excellent value.

  • Temperature: 25°C – 42°C
  • Significant hotel discounts
  • Fewer tourists and peaceful attractions

June — Seasonal Change

Beginning of Seasonal Change

June remains warm but occasional cloud cover starts appearing. Ideal for budget travelers, short weekend trips, and indoor attractions. Many visitors spend more time exploring museums, heritage properties, and cultural venues during this period.

  • Ideal for budget travelers and short weekend trips
  • Indoor attractions: museums, heritage properties, and cultural venues
  • Occasional cloud cover begins to appear

July — Monsoon Season

Monsoon Arrives

Rain transforms Udaipur's landscape. Unlike many Rajasthan destinations, Udaipur becomes greener and more scenic during the monsoon. Ideal for nature lovers, couples, and photographers.

  • Fresh air and green Aravalli Hills
  • Dramatic skies and beautiful photography opportunities
  • Ideal for nature lovers, couples, and photographers

August — Most Scenic

Udaipur at Its Most Scenic

Many experienced travelers consider August one of Udaipur's hidden gems. The city looks completely different — lakes appear fuller, hills become lush, and temperatures become comfortable.

  • Lakes appear fuller and hills become lush
  • Comfortable temperatures
  • Ideal for romantic trips, photography tours, and boutique travel experiences

September — End of Monsoon

End of Monsoon Beauty

September combines the best aspects of monsoon and winter. Visitors enjoy pleasant temperatures, green landscapes, and moderate crowds. An excellent time for heritage exploration, boat rides, and outdoor dining.

  • Pleasant temperatures and green landscapes
  • Moderate crowds
  • Excellent for heritage exploration, boat rides, and outdoor dining
By Travel Style

Best Time to Visit Udaipur
Based on Travel Style

The best time to visit Udaipur depends on what kind of experience you're looking for. Whether you're traveling as a family, a couple, a photographer, or on a budget, there's an ideal season for you.


Winter (October to March) offers pleasant weather, comfortable walking conditions, ideal lake experiences, better visibility, and cultural festivals. Most first-time visitors should plan their trip during these months.

For Families

Best Months: October, November, December, January, February. Comfortable temperatures make sightseeing easier for all age groups.

For Couples

Best Months: February, August, September, November. These months combine beautiful scenery with romantic weather conditions.

For Photography

Best Months: August, September, November, January. Monsoon landscapes and winter light provide exceptional photography opportunities.

For Budget Travelers

Best Months: April, May, June. Expect lower hotel prices and fewer crowds.

Seasonal Pricing

How Seasonal Demand
Affects Hotels in Udaipur

The time of year can significantly influence accommodation prices. Travelers looking for a heritage hotel in Udaipur often book several weeks in advance during winter, especially around holidays and festival periods.


For visitors seeking a more intimate experience, boutique and heritage hotels in Udaipur, including properties such as Kaladwas Lal Haveli, often provide a more authentic connection to the city's culture regardless of the season.

Peak Season — October to March

Expect higher demand, premium pricing, and the need for earlier bookings. Reserve well in advance during holidays and festival periods.

Off-Season — April to June

Benefits include better rates, greater availability, and fewer crowds. Ideal for budget-conscious travelers.

November to February

If you're visiting Udaipur for the first time, November to February offers the most comfortable weather and overall experience.

August and September

Choose August and September for lush landscapes and photography. A hidden gem for experienced travelers.
