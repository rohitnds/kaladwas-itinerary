20 Memorable Things to Do in Udaipur
Beyond Sightseeing

Udaipur is often celebrated for its grand palaces, shimmering lakes, and royal heritage. But beyond the iconic landmarks lies another side of the city—one filled with cultural encounters, hidden experiences, local traditions, and unforgettable moments.

While attractions like City Palace and Lake Pichola deserve their fame, the real magic of Udaipur often comes from slowing down and immersing yourself in the city's atmosphere.


If you're planning a trip to Udaipur, here are 20 memorable experiences that will help you connect more deeply with the City of Lakes.

01

Watch the Sunset from Lake Pichola

Few experiences capture the beauty of Udaipur quite like watching the sun set over Lake Pichola. As the sky changes color and historic palaces reflect on the water, the city feels almost timeless.

02

Take a Boat Ride at Golden Hour

A boat ride isn't just sightseeing—it's an experience. Cruising past Jag Mandir, historic ghats, and waterfront palaces offers a completely different perspective of the city.

03

Wander Through the Old City Without a Plan

Some of Udaipur's most memorable moments happen when you put away the map. The narrow lanes of the Old City reveal:

Hidden temples · Traditional homes · Local cafés · Artisan workshops · Historic havelis
04

Attend the Cultural Show at Bagore Ki Haveli

Bagore Ki Haveli hosts one of the most authentic cultural performances in Rajasthan. The evening show includes:

Folk dances · Puppet performances · Traditional music · Local storytelling
05

Enjoy a Rooftop Dinner Overlooking the Lakes

Udaipur is famous for its rooftop dining scene. An evening meal overlooking Lake Pichola, illuminated palaces, and the Aravalli skyline is an experience many travelers remember long after their trip ends.

06

Try Traditional Rajasthani Cuisine

A visit to Udaipur is incomplete without sampling local dishes. Food offers one of the best introductions to Rajasthan's culture.

Dal Baati Churma · Gatte Ki Sabzi · Ker Sangri · Laal Maas
07

Watch Sunrise at Fateh Sagar Lake

While most visitors head to the lake in the evening, mornings at Fateh Sagar Lake offer a quieter and more local experience. You'll find joggers, cyclists, tea vendors, and beautiful views of the surrounding hills.

08

Explore Local Markets

Markets provide a glimpse into everyday life. Look for handcrafted textiles, miniature paintings, and local souvenirs.

Hathipole Bazaar · Bapu Bazaar · Clock Tower Market
09

Learn About Miniature Painting

Udaipur is renowned for its miniature art tradition. Several workshops allow visitors to observe artists at work and understand the techniques passed down through generations.

10

Visit a Traditional Haveli

The city's historic havelis showcase intricate architecture, courtyards, and decorative details that tell the story of Mewar's heritage.

11

Take a Heritage Walk

Guided heritage walks help uncover stories that many visitors miss. These walks often explore:

Hidden temples · Historic neighborhoods · Royal architecture · Cultural traditions
12

Enjoy a Slow Evening at Gangaur Ghat

Gangaur Ghat is one of the most atmospheric places in the city. Sit by the water, watch local life unfold, and appreciate the slower pace that defines Udaipur.

13

Visit Shilpgram

Shilpgram offers an opportunity to experience rural Rajasthan through traditional crafts, performances, and artisan workshops.

14

Capture the City Through Photography

Udaipur is a paradise for photographers. Some of the best subjects include:

Lakeside reflections · Heritage architecture · Local markets · Sunset viewpoints · Historic streets
15

Explore Udaipur's Café Culture

The city has developed a vibrant café scene, particularly around the Old City and Lake Pichola. Many cafés combine excellent views with a relaxed atmosphere.

16

Take a Day Trip into the Aravalli Hills

The surrounding Aravalli landscape offers scenic drives, viewpoints, and a chance to experience rural Rajasthan beyond the city.

17

Watch the Sunset from Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace

Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace provides some of the most spectacular panoramic views in the region. The sunset experience here is often a highlight of any trip.

18

Experience a Heritage Stay

Accommodation can become part of the travel experience itself. Many visitors choose heritage hotels in Udaipur to immerse themselves in traditional architecture, local culture, and Rajasthan hospitality rather than simply using their hotel as a place to sleep.

19

Connect with Local Artisans

Whether it's painting, textile work, pottery, or handicrafts, meeting artisans provides a deeper appreciation for the craftsmanship that defines Rajasthan.

20

Simply Slow Down and Absorb the Atmosphere

Perhaps the most memorable thing to do in Udaipur is also the simplest. Udaipur rewards travelers who slow down.

Sitting by the lake · Watching daily life · Exploring quiet streets · Enjoying conversations · Appreciating the city's rhythm
